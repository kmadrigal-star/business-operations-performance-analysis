# Business Operations Performance Analysis

## Overview
This project analyzes operational performance data from a small business environment to support process improvement and operational decision-making.

## Data
- Operational metrics related to staffing, scheduling, and customer demand
- Internally tracked business data

## Analysis
- Trend analysis of operational performance
- Evaluation of staffing and capacity alignment
- Performance reporting

## Tools
- Excel
- Google Sheets

## Results
- Identified workflow inefficiencies
- Supported data-driven operational adjustments
- Improved visibility into performance metrics

## Next Steps
- Automate reporting dashboards
- Incorporate additional operational KPIs
